NeighborLink

Features Benefits Case Studies Roadmap

Building Stronger Neighborhoods

Connect, share, and collaborate with your neighbors to build a more sustainable and socially connected community.

Key Features

Communal Goods

List and borrow tools, furniture, and items within your community.

Skill/Service Exchanges

Connect with neighbors offering services like tutoring or repairs.

Neighborhood Events

Discover and participate in workshops, meetups, and local activities.

Benefits to the Community

Reduced Waste

Borrow instead of buying to reduce unnecessary purchases.

Sustainability

Sharing promotes eco-friendly living and conscious consumption.

Social Bonds

Build stronger relationships with your neighbors through shared experiences.

User Case Studies

Tool Sharing

Borrow a drill for repairs or lend gardening tools to neighbors.

Skill-Building Workshops

Host or attend workshops on cooking, crafts, or tech skills.

Future Roadmap

  • New Features: Enhance engagement with innovative tools.
  • Scalability: Expand to more neighborhoods and cities.
  • Impact Measurement: Track community improvements and engagement.
