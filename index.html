Connect. Share. Empower.

NeighborLink helps communities share tools, services, and opportunities, fostering trust and sustainability.

Share Tools

Lend or borrow gardening tools, DIY kits, and more with trusted neighbors.

Offer Services

Teach music, repair electronics, or help with resumes—connect through your skills.

Event Hub

Stay informed about block parties, clean-up drives, and local initiatives.

Community Support

Find or offer support during emergencies and collaborate on shared needs.

Trusted Network

Build real-life reputation through reviews and verifications, ensuring safe exchanges.

Marketplace

Buy and sell local handmade goods, upcycled furniture, or vintage finds.

Platform Features

Smart Inventory System

Manage shared tools and appliances easily. Users can check availability, reserve items, and track usage history.

Skill Exchange Listings

Find local experts and offer your skills. Ratings and reviews help maintain quality and trust.

Neighborhood Calendar

Track upcoming community events and RSVP online. Stay involved and never miss a local happening.

Push Notifications

Get instant alerts about item return deadlines, event updates, and safety announcements.

Mobile-First Design

Access all features on the go with a seamless mobile interface that supports accessibility standards.

Case Studies

Urban Garden Revival – Springfield

Neighbors collaborated to convert an abandoned lot into a thriving community garden using shared tools and knowledge.

Senior Tech Buddy Program – Rivertown

Tech-savvy teens offered tutoring for seniors, helping them use smartphones and stay digitally connected.

Tool-Time Trust – Lakeside

Residents organized monthly sharing drives for tools and books, drastically cutting consumer waste.
